About

I am a Simulation & Embedded Systems Engineer specializing in real-time training and simulation systems with over 5 years of experience across Defence and Railway domains. My work focuses on designing and developing complete simulation ecosystems using Unity and Unreal Engine, including distributed instructor–trainee architectures, real-time physics-driven environments, radar and electronic warfare simulation (ECM/ECCM), and hardware–software integration. I have contributed to multiple large-scale simulators including driving, firing, and operational training systems built on real-time communication frameworks with automated evaluation and reporting engines. I also design embedded and hardware-integrated systems such as sensor-based detection modules, servo-driven gauge systems, and control hardware integrated with simulation platforms. My core interest lies in building high-fidelity simulation, game, and real-time interactive systems, combining software, system architecture, and hardware integration to deliver complete simulation products.
